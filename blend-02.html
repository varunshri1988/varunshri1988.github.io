The Blend

Blend No. 02 —
Bold

Not mild. Not balanced. Just bold. A full-bodied blend crafted for those who love strong chai.

Blend No. 02 — Bold brings together three carefully selected Assam CTC teas — chosen for their malty depth, bold body, strength, and their ability to hold character through milk and heat — with a fourth component we have chosen not to name. Four leaves. One cup. No compromise.

We blend in small batches. Not months in advance. Close enough to when it ships that what reaches you is fresh tea at its peak — not tea that has been sitting in a supply chain catching stale air.

Every pack is carefully sealed to preserve freshness, ensuring a rich, full-bodied cup with pronounced strength and depth. Designed especially for those who brew strong — with milk, with spices, or black.

One teaspoon. One to two minutes minimum. Three minutes for full strength.
